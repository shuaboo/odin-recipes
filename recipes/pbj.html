Description

Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich is a staple food item popular amongst schoolchildren that is simple to make, yet beloved by many. It involves your choice of Peanut Butter and Jelly between two slices of bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Fruit Jelly

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Take a knife and spread peanut butter along one slice of bread
  3. Clean off the peanut butter residue and repeat the process with jam on the other slice of bread
  4. Optional: Slice off the crusts of the bread
  5. stack smothered bread and enjoy
